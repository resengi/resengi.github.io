How we put our values into practice

Three values, three commitments that prove them.

Honesty

Clear, direct communication at every stage of the build.

We tell you the truth about timelines, costs, and architecture upfront. If there is a better way to build something, we'll say so. If something isn't working, you'll hear it from us first.

Transparency

Published financials, externally reviewed.

Our books are public. Anyone can see what we earn, what we spend, and what margin we make. Our books will be externally reviewed by a CPA each quarter once client work begins.

Fairness

Cost-based pricing, not market-based pricing.

We show our cost breakdown and margin upfront. If a project comes in under budget, you pay less. If it goes over, we absorb the difference.
