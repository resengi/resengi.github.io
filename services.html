What we do

We partner with startups and growing companies to turn complex ideas into production-ready software. From cross-platform mobile applications to custom AI pipelines, we provide the technical heavy lifting that makes your product real.

Our services

App Development

From idea to shipped product. We build mobile and web applications for clients who know what they need but need a team to make it real. We work across platforms and tech stacks, choosing the right tools for the problem.

AI Engineering

We help companies build AI-powered features, develop custom pipelines, and integrate language models and other AI systems into their products. We focus on solutions that work reliably in production and are built to last.

Prototype to Production

Have an MVP, a proof of concept, or an early prototype that works but is not production-ready? We specialize in taking early-stage software and bringing it to the level of quality, security, and scalability it needs to serve real users.

Who we work best with

We work best with people who care about how their software is built and want an honest picture of what it will actually cost. Whether you are an early-stage founder with a rough prototype, or a growing team solving a complex AI challenge, we value partners who appreciate plain speaking and fair dealing.

How we price

Our pricing model is straightforward. We estimate the cost of a project (hours, contractor rates, tooling, etc.) add a fair, disclosed margin, and present the full breakdown to the client before work begins.

If a project comes in under budget, you pay less. If it comes in over budget, we absorb the difference. You will always know what you are paying for and why.

This approach means our margins may be lower and more variable than a typical agency's. We accept this trade-off because we prioritize building sustainable, transparent partnerships over maximizing short-term margins.

You can see our actual financial results on our Financials page.

How to get started

If you have a project in mind, reach out. We will have an honest conversation about what you need, whether we are the right fit, and what it would cost. If we are not the right fit, we will tell you and, where appropriate, refer you to someone we trust.
